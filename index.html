I'm a philosopher of ethics and AI. I recently completed my doctorate at the University of Oxford. From August 2025, I will be a Postdoctoral Associate at Cornell Tech as part of the Digital Life Initiative.

My research is guided by questions about artificial moral agency—that is, whether AI systems are genuine moral agents (capable of acting morally wrongly and responsibly) and why we should care.
