I'm a Postdoctoral Fellow at Cornell Tech (Cornell University), affiliated with the Digital Life Initiative. I'm a philosopher of ethics and AI.

My research is guided by questions about artificial moral agency—that is, whether AI systems are genuine moral agents (capable of acting morally wrongly and responsibly) and why we should care.

I got my DPhil (PhD) in Philosophy from the University of Oxford. I have an MPhil in Philosophy from the University of Cambridge, an MA in Medieval Icelandic Studies from the University of Iceland, and a BA in Philosophy and Public Policy from Duke University.
