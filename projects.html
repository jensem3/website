DPhil Project


My dissertation is provisionally titled "Artificial Non-sentient Moral Agency." In it, I explore several interrelated theoretical and practical questions about AI and moral decision-making. I consider whether it's possible (conceptually and technoloigcally) for an artificial entity to be a full moral agentâ€”and I explore some implications for the role of AI in the moral community. I am also interested in how future AIs might put pressure on the relationship between moral agency and moral responsibility. I have working drafts of the following chapters:

  1. The Concept of Moral Agency
  2. Artificial Non-sentient Moral Agency
  3. AI and Moral Agency
